Buyer safety tips

Before you confirm an order, make sure the item and delivery details match what you expected.

  • check_circleCheck seller ratings and reviews before placing an order.
  • check_circleRead product descriptions, condition notes, and delivery details carefully.
  • check_circleDon't confirm delivery until you've received the correct item.
  • check_circleOpen a dispute if something goes wrong with the order.

Seller safety tips

Build trust by being clear, responsive, and accurate from listing to delivery confirmation.

  • check_circleUse accurate product photos and honest descriptions.
  • check_circleKeep buyers updated about delivery or collection status.
  • check_circleOnly mark an order fulfilled once delivery or collection is complete.
  • check_circleRespond to buyer questions quickly and professionally.
